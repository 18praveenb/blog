  • Hollow Knight
    • The first time I played this game, I gave up shortly after Greenpath and scored it a 6/10. I had been hoping for a boss rush and was stuck backtracking from benches, dying, and losing my Geo.
    • When I came back to play Hollow Knight in preparation for Silksong, I was ready for the exploration, and my biggest concern was that the world would feel too lifeless. Thankfully, there are quite a few NPCs you can run into and chat with during your journey, and I had a much better time (though once I got far enough along in my Silksong playthrough, it was hard to go back to Hollow Knight bosses and deal with the less forgiving dash and heal mechanisms).
